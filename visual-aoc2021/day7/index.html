Day 7

The Treachery of Whales

The task is to align a swarm of crabs to a common horizontal position, while spending as little fuel as possible. Read the full description of the puzzle at Advent of Code

Each pink dot in the visualisation represents a crab. To find the optimal position for the swarm, we calculate the median of all crabs position, visualised by the vertical line.

The cost for every crab to move to that position is the difference between the crabs current position and the median position.
